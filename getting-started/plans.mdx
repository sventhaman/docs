  </Step>

## How usage is billed

Your subscription covers the platform. **Usage** — what your agents actually spend talking and chatting — is billed separately, in dollars.

- The balance is labeled **Credits**, but every number is a real `$` amount.
- Each plan includes a monthly usage allowance: **Starter $25 · Growth $75 · Scale $150** (the trial is capped at $5).
- Go over the allowance and usage bills as overage. You can also buy one-off **top-ups** or turn on **auto-reload** so agents never pause mid-conversation.
